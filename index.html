Do you know what is the difference between an income for a bank deposit and an investment fund with the same parameters?

In my past business of finance, I've noticed that many people can't accurately calculate or compare the return on a term deposit and a mutual fund, where we have the same parameters, namely the amount, the rate of return and the duration. Therefore, I combined my previous experience in the financial industry along with my acquired skills in programming and decided to create this simple application/tool for you. If you want to quickly count the difference in profit, you will do it with this tool, try it and count it!
